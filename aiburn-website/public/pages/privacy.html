AIBurn is built on one principle: your data is yours alone.

  • Your API key is never stored. It's used once to fetch your data, then immediately discarded.

1. Introduction

AIBurn ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the AIBurn calculator tool (the "Service").

2. Zero Personal Data Collection

AIBurn does not collect, store, or retain any personal data about you. We do not:

  • Store your name, email, or contact information
  • Track your location or device identifiers
  • Use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes
  • Create user accounts or profiles
  • Log your IP address for analytics

3. API Key Handling

When you use the "Exact Usage" calculator feature:

  • Your API key is never stored. It is transmitted directly to our secure server via encrypted HTTPS connection only to fetch your usage data from OpenAI's API.
  • After we retrieve your usage information, the API key is immediately discarded and not stored in any database or logs.
  • API key transmissions are encrypted and comply with industry security standards.
  • You remain fully responsible for keeping your API key confidential. Do not share it with others.

4. Usage Data

The "Quick Calculator" mode does not transmit any data. The "Exact Usage" mode sends only:

  • Your API key (for authentication with OpenAI)
  • The retrieved usage statistics from your OpenAI account

These transactions are temporary and not retained in our systems.

5. Cookies

AIBurn does not use tracking cookies. Your browser session data is managed locally in your browser memory and is cleared when you close the tab.

6. Third-Party Services

AIBurn may transmit your API key to OpenAI's API to retrieve your usage data. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy for their data practices.

7. Advertising

AIBurn displays advertisements. Advertisement partners may collect limited data according to their own privacy policies. We do not control third-party advertising networks' data collection practices.

8. Compliance
